Eve


Introduction

Eve is a bio mechanical engineer for the US Government. She worked for 2 decades developing technology that allowed soldiers injured in battle to gain bio-mechanical limbs which not only restored their bodies but enhanced their speed, reflexes and strength to make them better soldiers. Eventually, Eve began a new set of research that would allow mechanical devices that could restore and enhance senses such as site and hearing to allow those with disabilities to to serve in combat and provide the government with a wider pool of soldiers. Just as she was reaching a breakthrough discovery, Dr. Eve began to experience headaches, light sensitivity and vision lost. After loosing her ability to stand bright light at all, Dr. Eve went into the infirmary on base to discover that she had a degenerative eye disease that would eventually take away her sight completely. Determined to take this matter into her own hands, Eve worked tirelessly to create a prototype of the glasses she'd invented to restore vision. When she was finished she cautiously tested the device on her own face. Once activated, Dr. Eve was horrified to find that the glasses were emitting a strange purple smoke and felt like they were cemented to her face. Panicked, she looked around the lab for a way to pry them off but then realized that the room appeared pitch black but she could see everything. Her vision had no been restored but somehow she was able to create darkness her and therefore be able to see using night vision through the glasses.







Name/Title: Eve

Height/Weight: 5'4"/140

Super Power: Dark Mist

Learned Skills: Voice Replication, Bio-Engineering

Enhanced Abilities: Enhanced Hearing, Enhanced Reflexes, Enhanced Night-Vision
