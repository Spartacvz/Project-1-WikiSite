Leo


Introduction

Leo is a human from an alternate earth, his story begins with his village being massacred by the local tyrant. Leo loses his parents and six brother's during the raid. His entire village is burnt down leaving little more than the pit him and few other children hid in. After the raid Leo surveys the land where his family's hut once stood, and found nothing but the swords belonging to his father, brothers and mother surrounding the area. Leo and the other children live out of the pit for some time scavaging for food only when the coast is clear. Eventually Leo is forced to lead the kids away from the scorched lands because food has grown scarce due to the lack of vegetation and the growing needs of the children. During the children's travels they stumble upon a group of monks who take them in and care for them. All of the children are content living a peaceful conflict free existence except for Leo who is still troubled by the incident that cost him his family. Leo spends all of his time working through his anger by learning the martial arts from the monks. Eventually Leo becomes known as the juggling swordsman.
