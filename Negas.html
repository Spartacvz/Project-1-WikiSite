Negas


Introduction

Negas' story begins far away on the planet Segan. The planet Segan used to be a world of many Nations until a tyrant, nicknamed the dawg beast, took over and destroyed everything. From the ashes of destruction rose a new mild mannered civilization solely focused on enjoying and preserving life.

Negas, also known as the dawg beast, Negas is from another planet in which his family are the elites (royalty) and he's the outcast. A totalitarian monarchy is what rules the planet of Segan. All war and most violence had been stamped out thousands of years before thanks to the tyrannical rule of an emperor known as the Dawg Beast! Negas' last day on his planet he rescues his brother from the rebellion by awakening the spirit of the dog beast. In doing so he becomes banned from his home planet.







Name/Title: Negas "The Dawg Beast"

Height/Weight: 5'8"/160

Super Power: Fire

Learned Skills: Metal-working, Mechanical Engineer

Enhanced Abilities: Durable Skin (fire/acid proof), Enhanced Reflexes, Thermal vision
