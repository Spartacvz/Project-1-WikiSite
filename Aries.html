Aries


Introduction

Aries is from a different realm, similar to earth. His father is currently the emperor and his grand-father was the general before the current general. His grand-father was the general that conquered the planet, but there are currently uprisings in the regions. Aries was originally training to be a spy for his father but when his father won the final battle it was no longer needed. Aries then changed career paths and set his sights on becoming the next general. Every three years anyone in the military ranks are granted the opportunity to compete for promotion. The competition starts out with three contest that test an individual’s military skills (marksmanship, hand-to-hand combat, combat strategy) Each contest has three sections, anyone in the lead after the final contest has come to a conclusion may challenge the current general for his rank This challenge is carried out by way of a duel which usually results in the death of one competitor. When Aries is pulled from his world he is in the middle of this challenge.
